Privacy Policy for SeClip

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which kuroappworks ("we", "us", or "our") handles user information in the mobile application "SeClip" (the "App").

Article 1: Information We Collect and Its Purpose

1. Information We Do Not Collect Directly

The App does not collect any personally identifiable information (such as name, email address, or contact information) or sensitive user data (such as precise location from GPS, camera, microphone, photos, or health data). All data you register within the App, including titles, fields, and custom entries, is stored only on your local device. This data is never transmitted to or stored on external servers by us.

2. Information Collected by Third-Party Services (Ad Delivery)

The App uses the following third-party service for the purpose of delivering advertisements. This service may automatically collect certain information as described below. The collected information is managed according to the privacy policy of the service provider.

Service Name: Google AdMob

Purpose: To display advertisements, measure ad performance, and analyze usage patterns.

Information Collected (Example): Device identifiers, IP address, coarse location data, and advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID).

Provider: Google LLC

Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Article 2: Purpose of Use of Information

The information collected indirectly through third-party services (i.e., Google AdMob) is used for the following purposes:

To deliver, display, and measure the effectiveness of advertisements.

To analyze the usage of the App to help us improve its quality and functionality (e.g., through AdMob's usage reports).

Article 3: Backup and Restore Functionality

The App provides a backup and restore feature that allows you to export and import your data.

Data Handling: The backup process is initiated entirely by you. The backup file is created on your device's storage in a location you specify. This file contains the data you have registered in the App, including encrypted data from the "Secure" tab.

User Responsibility: We are not involved in the storage, management, or transmission of your backup files. You are solely responsible for managing your backup files securely. Please do not share them with third parties or store them in unsecured locations, as they may contain sensitive information.

Article 4: Provision of Information to Third Parties

We will not provide collected information to third parties (excluding Google AdMob) without your consent, except as required by law.

Article 5: Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy in response to changes in laws, regulations, or our services. If we make any material changes, we will notify you through the App or on our website in an easily understandable manner.
